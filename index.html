About MN Shroom

At MN Shroom, we are passionate about bringing the finest quality processed mushrooms to your table. Located in the heart of Minnesota, we specialize in sustainable cultivation, meticulous processing, and efficient distribution of a wide variety of mushrooms.

Our commitment to quality begins at the source, working closely with local growers who share our values for organic and environmentally friendly practices. We employ state-of-the-art processing techniques to ensure freshness, flavor, and nutritional integrity in every batch.

From gourmet varieties for culinary enthusiasts to bulk supplies for food service, MN Shroom is dedicated to meeting the diverse needs of our customers with excellence and reliability.
